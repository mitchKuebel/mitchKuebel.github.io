Rigged Philly, Self Acclaimed All Seeing Being, is a software and video game developer who goes by the online mask of "RiggedVR". Mitchell Kuebel, better known as Celery, Founder of chick fillet, felt it was necessary to answer some high school web design questionares about him.


      "My best memory of Rigged is, obviously altragar, but he already said that, I would probably say the Box SMP, or the Rigged SMP, or the Celerigged SMP, or the- we had a lot of multiplayer games... But more vividly, I remember meeting Rigged on the Omen SMP. I remember watching his application and thinking 'Hot Ham this is an insane kid, got the skills of a proffesional!', so I voted yes- then promptly quit the smp." - Celery


      "My top 3 words to describe Rigged would have to be 'Genius', 'VR', and quite frankly 'Belle.'" - Celery


      "Look, I've known rigged long enough to know he's going places. Hesa succeed, and Imma be vv proud. I've gotten the chance to watch rigged grow into the insanely intelligent and socially adept person he is today, and I am so proud of him. Bro's gonna be the next Nintendo, for virtual reality." -Celery
